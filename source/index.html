About

What is a product designer?

Product Designers try to make things that people want. We investigate and validate user problems, invent and test potential solutions, and ultimately design and ship products or features. We are generalists with competency in multiple disciplines, including user research, testing, interaction design, visual design, prototyping and software development.

What's your professional background?

Eclectic! I've worked on hardware, educational products, web projects, and most recently, consumer software and mobile apps (iOS). I provide product design and development services to clients while pursuing side projects and entrepreurial efforts. No great succeses yet but I’ll never quit trying.

Education?

I have a BA in Environmental Economics from UCSC, where I achieved high honors for undergraduate thesis work on municipal solar projects. It took me six years to graduate because I worked full time through college (getting my real education) at a medium-sized product development consultancy. That’s where I learned foundational design skills, team work and how to ship products.

More recently I've attended software development training to increase technical skills. I completed Dev Bootcamp in 2014, where I learned Ruby on Rails, and Mobile Makers Academy in 2015, where I learned iOS development.
